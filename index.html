Enable Location Access

To ensure your safety during emergencies, please enable location services on your device.

📱 For Safari users: Please set location access to "While Using the App" when prompted.

This allows responders to track your location in real-time during an emergency.

⚠️ Important: During an SOS, please keep this app open and your screen on for continuous location tracking. The app will pause updates if your phone goes to sleep or you switch to other apps.

You can always enable location later in your browser settings.
